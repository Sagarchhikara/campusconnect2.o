Frequently Asked Questions

What is Campus Connect?

Campus Connect is a platform designed to help students connect with academic and extracurricular opportunities within their university.

How can I register on Campus Connect?

You can register by signing up with your university email and verifying your identity through the provided verification process.

What features does Campus Connect offer?

Campus Connect provides networking opportunities, event updates, study groups, and academic resources tailored to your university.

Is Campus Connect free to use?

Yes, the platform is free for students and faculty members of registered universities.

How can I post an event on Campus Connect?

Registered users can submit event details through the event submission form, which will be reviewed before being published.
